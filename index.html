cor·nu·co·pi·a

ˌkôrn(y)əˈkōpēə/

noun
  1. a symbol of plenty consisting of a goat's horn overflowing with flowers, fruit, and corn.
  • an ornamental container shaped line a goat's horn.
  • an abundant supply of good things of a specified kind.

No, this podcast isn't about goat horns filled with flowers. But, I guess it could be since this is a totally random podcast.

We are about the abundance of innovative, talented folks, who happen to be our neighbors and friends. Our mission is to give those who feel not heard a chance to share their ideas, stories, thoughts, jokes, etc.

Whatever comes to the ol' noggin!
